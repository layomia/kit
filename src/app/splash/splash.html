How it works

You can donate items to us and let us know what cause you want the money to go towards. People will be able to make offers and you can select whichever offer you like. You can also buy cheap items from within your community, knowing that the money you spend is going towards causes in your community. All money from our platform is re-invested into your community, to the issues you care about the most.
